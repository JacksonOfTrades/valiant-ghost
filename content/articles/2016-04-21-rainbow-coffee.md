Coffee is typically that lovely brown color which I strive for first thing in the morning, but turns out multicolored coffee is cool too as Mason Salisbury has shown me [@ibrewcoffee](https://www.instagram.com/ibrewcoffee/). Using food dye[^1] he has created a new art form which allows for some very cool, very colorful looking coffee. It's used to train baristas, but **dam*n* I want some now.
